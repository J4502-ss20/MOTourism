    >West Winery- visitors walking into the West winery on a sunny day in Macon have to wait a moment for their eyes to adjust in the low light environment. once they can see exposed brick walls, dark wooden floors and Stained Glass windows at the end of the room combined to create an urban industrial feel in a small town setting. The tasting room and winery are all Housed under the same roof in a long narrow building downtown. just past the bar and down two steps rest for large vats where the wine is stored. stained glass sits above the vats a light at the end of the tunnel. hanging above the long dark stained wooden bar are the wine options written on chalkboards.
