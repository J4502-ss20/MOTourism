    >Flowers & Weeds-Flowers & Weeds a small shop on Cherokee Street immerses visitors into a lighthearted world of plants. there’s green everywhere from the rows of baby succulents on the walls to the leafy house plants on the floor to the glistening glass terrariums hanging from the ceiling. customers can discover pots shaped like unicorns, dinosaurs and elephants as well as other garden related items such as crystals, ceramics, candles and more. if they’re really lucky they might even notice the two cats that have made the plant shop their home. Flowers & Weeds sells much more than plants it calls itself a combination floral studio urban flower grower and greenhouse.
